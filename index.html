Quick zoo tips

  • Bring water and food with you!
  • Bring a portable charger for electronics!
  • Wear comfortable shoes!
  • Bring a hat and sunscreen!

Important Notice

We provide our own food for the animals for a good price! Do not bring your own food, as it can hurt the animals
